How it works

Brand first.
Intelligence second.

Mirra starts by understanding your brand, goals, and market — so every insight is tailored to your specific competitive position.

Step 01

Tell us about your brand

60-second setup. Your product, audience, goals, and market. Mirra uses this to make every insight relevant to you — not just generic observations.

Step 02

Scan your competitors

Paste any URL. Mirra renders the page, labels every component with AI, and maps their positioning, gaps, and strengths in seconds.

Step 03

Get your strategic playbook

Steal, exploit, or watch — every insight is framed against your brand. Export as PDF, share with your team, or ask the AI strategist follow-up questions.
